What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

AI powers technologies like chatbots, self-driving cars, facial recognition, and smart assistants. It is rapidly transforming how we work and interact with technology.

Governments and businesses across the world are investing heavily in AI due to its ability to automate complex tasks, analyze massive data, and make predictions.

As AI evolves, discussions around ethics, transparency, and human oversight are becoming more important than ever before.
